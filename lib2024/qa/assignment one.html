Goncharov

The Greatest Mafia Movie Ever Made

Produced by Martin Scorsese in 1973, Goncharov follows Robert De Niro as the titular character, who is a Russian hitman and former discotheque mmanager. Goncharov is a story of love, murder, and running out of time... All set to the beautiful backdrop of Naples.

Cast List:

  • Robert De Niro as Lo Straniero/Goncharov
  • Cybill Shepherd as Katya Michailov Goncharov
  • Harvey Keitel as Andrey 'The Banker' Daddano
  • Gene Hackman as Valery Michailov
  • John Cazale as Joseph 'Ice Pick Joe' Morelli
  • Sophia Loren as Sophie
  • Al Pacino as Mario Ambrosini
  • Patchka the Cat as himself
