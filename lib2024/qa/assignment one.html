My Favourite Movie: Goncharov (1973)

The Greatest Mafia Movie Ever Made

Produced by Martin Scorsese in 1973, Goncharov follows Robert De Niro as the titular character, who is a Russian hitman and former discotheque mmanager, and his wife Katya. They cannot escape the connection they have with each other, or the connections they each have with Andrey and Sophie. Goncharov is a story of love, murder, and betrayal... All set to the beautiful backdrop of Naples.

Cast List:

  • Robert De Niro as Lo Straniero/Goncharov
  • Cybill Shepherd as Katya Michailov Goncharov
  • Harvey Keitel as Andrey 'The Banker' Daddano
  • Gene Hackman as Valery Michailov
  • John Cazale as Joseph 'Ice Pick Joe' Morelli
  • Sophia Loren as Sophie
  • Al Pacino as Mario Ambrosini
  • Patchka the Cat as himself
