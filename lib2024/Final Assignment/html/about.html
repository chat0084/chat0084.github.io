a lil' about me

Hi, my name is Pigeon!

Birthday: April 4, 2018
Species: Cat
Colour: White
Eye colour: Green and blue

I'm Pigeon and I was adopted by Grey in July 2020. I have a loud voice and I'm not afraid to use it! I love to meow at 3am when I'm hungry or bored. My favourite place to sleep is in the papasan or on my perch by the window. I love watching the birds, and squirrels, and bunny rabbits through the window. If only I could get my hands on them... I love salmon bisque for lunch, and Cheetos as a snack. Whenever sommeone opens a bag of Cheetos, I come running! I'll then stare at them until they let me lick the dust off their Cheeto.

I love to sit on people's laps when they watch TV. But never pick me. I hate being picked up. And I hate going to the vet. It smells weird there and I don't trust those people in the scrubs! I love it when my best friend, Rollo, comes to visit. We'll play and run around the house at all hours of the night. I let him eat some of my tasty food since his is yucky! Sometimes he hides from me in the laundry room. I don't go in there... It's dark and scary. But Rollo is so brave!
